Tim O’Reilly in a Nutshell

Introduction

A preacher. That’s what the high school aptitude test told Tim O’Reilly he should be. Possessed of a yearning to understand, a passion for doing the right thing, the desire to spread the good word, and the skill to persuade, Tim was a prime candidate for the ministry As it happens, he ended up preaching the gospel of technology.

Tim is also, fundamentally, a writer. In the twenty-five years he’s spent immersed in the computer technology world, Tim has often turned to writing as a tool to understand, and to share what he discovers. Tim O’Reilly in a Nutshell is a collection of Tim’s most thought-provoking pieces about technology, the publishing business, and life (arranged by theme rather than date). For more of Tim’s writing, see tim.oreilly.com.
